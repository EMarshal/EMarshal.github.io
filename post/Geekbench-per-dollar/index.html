Geekbench per dollar

A comparison of the Geekbench performance of mobile devices compared to their cost. Have tried to normalize price by preferentially choosing 32GB, then 64GB, then 16GB models, depending on availability. I’ve only included devices that are readily available unlocked, without carrier control over software updates. The Moto X Play (Snapdragon 615) and Galaxy S6 (Exynos 7420) included as exceptions based on their interesting SoCs. Windows phones excluded as Geekbench isn’t available.
