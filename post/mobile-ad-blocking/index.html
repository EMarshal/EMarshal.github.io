mobile ad blocking

2015-19-19: Updated to reflect currently available ad blockers.

This isn’t meant to be a comprehensive guide, but rather a summary of the best available options.

Device Independent

Use a hosts file on your linux-based router of choice to block ads across your home network.

Android

Use the Adblock Browser for Android from ABP. If your device is rooted, use a hosts file.

iOS

Use the Adblock Browser from ABP. I do not currently recommend any of the currently available content blockers for Safari, they are all closed source with a lack of attribution as to the origin of the filter list contents.

Windows Phone

You’re hooped. See device independent ad blocking. Hopefully with Windows 10 and Microsoft Edge, extension support for the browser will come to mobile and uBlock Origin can be ported over.
