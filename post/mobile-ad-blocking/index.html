Mobile Ad Blocking

This isn’t meant to be a comprehensive guide, but rather a summary of the best available options. (They’re not great, but indications are that they’ll be improving in the near future.)

Device Independent

Use a hosts file on your linux-based router of choice to block ads across your home network.

Android

Use the Adblock Browser for Android from ABP. Other options suffer from the same problem as iOS below. If your device is rooted, use a hosts file.

iOS

Current options are all poor due to various issues, including only working on Wi-Fi, needing low-quality custom browsers, requiring jailbreak or fiddling with DNS or VPN settings. Wait for ABP to release an iOS solution.

Windows Phone

You’re hooped. See device independent ad blocking. Hopefully with Windows 10 and Microsoft Edge, extension support for the browser will come to mobile and uBlock can be ported over.
